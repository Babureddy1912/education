I struck and struck again,(1)

And growing still(2) in stature the grim shape(3)

Towered up between me and the stars, and still,

For so it seemed,(4) with purpose of its own

And measured motion(5) like a living thing,

Strode after me.

Comments

(1) Rowed quickly – repetition shows panic

(2) Feels relentless – it won’t give up, which is why he turns and flees

(3) Mountain seems to be gett ing bigger – due to the effects of perspective but to him it looks threatening

(4) Here, though, looking back and saying that it only seemed like this

(5) The measured motion is like that of a human or animal taking regular steps. Use of personification – the mountain peaks have become threatening beings, like monsters.
