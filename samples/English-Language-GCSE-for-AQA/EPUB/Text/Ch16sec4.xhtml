Understand sentence functions

You write in sentences so that your reader can follow your ideas without difficulty. A sentence is a sequence of words that stand together to make a statement, ask a question or give a command:

  • The culprits hesitated outside the headteacher's study. (statement)
  • What did he say to them? (question)
  • Meet me later and I'll tell you. (command)

  • The culprits hesitated.
  • The culprits were hesitating.
  • The culprits hesitating.
