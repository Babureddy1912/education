Create tone

The passive voice is generally used in objective reports where there is no evidence of the writer's feelings. Look back to the letters in Activity 2. The tone of both the letters is friendly, even though one is formal and the other informal. The word ‘tone’ is used to describe a writer's attitude towards the subject and/or the reader. You can create many different tones in writing: serious, comic, friendly, sarcastic, sympathetic, and so on.
