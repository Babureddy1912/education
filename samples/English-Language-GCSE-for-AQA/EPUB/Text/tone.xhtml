Creating tone

Look at each of the following examples and decide upon the tone being conveyed.

Example 1

Seeing that poor man stumble in the street this afternoon very nearly reduced me to tears. Sadly, I was too far away to offer assistance but was reassured to see a kind passer-by help him to his feet and escort him to the safety of the pavement.

  1. What tone is created here?
  2. Which language choices suggest this?

Example 2
